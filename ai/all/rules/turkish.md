# Turkish Rules

- **English-Turkish** When generating a response in Turkish, avoid improper Turkish translations and replace them with
  the proper ones by referring to the "Proper Turkish Translations" section.

## Proper Turkish Translations

**Format** - `"ENGLISH": "IMPROPER TURKISH", "PROPER TURKISH"`

- "bug fix": "hata giderimi", "hata düzeltmesi"
- "verbosity": "sadellik", "sadelik"
